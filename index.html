Procurement Rules

The Laptop Purchase Window will strictly open between May 1 and May 10, 2026, provided the 13k fund is met.
  • Financial Clear: Minimum ₹13,000 in Central Fund.
  • March Audit: Upload ARATS calendar. AI must verify a minimum of 35% daily activity completion.
  • April Audit: Upload ARATS calendar. AI must verify a strict minimum of 66% daily activity.
  • Holiday Rule: A maximum of 7 days of non-compliance (Holidays) are exempt across both months.
  • NOC: Upon successful AI scan and Affidavit check, generate the Official NOC.
  • Final Permission: Upload the NOC back into the portal for Executive Magistrate verification to receive the final purchase order.
